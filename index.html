Welcome to History Helper!

If you have a previous patient coming in again for surgery, a consult and stay, or a recheck, this automates filling in the blanks by utilizing a previous history.

Instructions:
1. Fill in the History Helper form up until the "Old History" section.

2. Go to your patient in ezyVet and open the "Complete History" tab on the left.

3. Scroll through the "History" section and find their most recent history. Open it and click on the textbox containing the history. Hit Ctrl-A to select all, then Ctrl-C to copy. Do not select/copy the history from outside of the history edit window.

4. Return to History Helper. Click on the "Old History" textbox and hit Ctrl-V to paste.

5. Select the type of new history desired, then click "Submit". Output should fill with your new history. Click the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy.

6. Return to ezyVet. Create a new history and hit Ctrl-V to paste the output into the history textbox.

7. Hit the "Clear Form" button to start things over for your next patient.

8. Great success!

If you notice any errors or bugs... you know who to call.
